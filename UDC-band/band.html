ABOUT US

The ACE OF SPADES is a rockband which belongs to the underdogs community, founded by Pranjal kulshreshtha. The members Shikhar malhotra , Deepti yadav , Naman Jain , Sankhayan Ganguly and Deepam Kumar , excel in their respective forte. When they perform together as a group , their passion for music , teamwork and dedication is displayed. With energetic beats and synchronized rhythm , they can pull the crowd and steal each heart. The talented and lively members of the band are everready to take you by the edge of your seats.
